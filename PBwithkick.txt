Recipe for making a "Peanut Butter Sandwich with a kick" (Inspired from NY Times)

1. Choose choice of bread.
3. Keep pickles and sriracha or another hot sauce handy.
2. Take 2 slices and toast them in a toaster or a pan.
3. After they are toasted, apply peanut butter on one side of each slice respectively.
4. Pick any one slice and take some pickles and arrange on the side where the peanut butter was applied. 
5. Squeeze some sriracha or hot sauce on the arranged pickles.
6. Apply the other bread slice with just peanut butter side on top of the one which was arranged with pickles and sauce.
7. Enjoy the sandwich!
